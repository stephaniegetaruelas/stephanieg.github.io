My safe haven

This section will provide an overview of the people who nurtured me, saw my best, and helped me gain confidence in this world.

Family

Eva Burrows once quoted that, “The love of a family is life’s greatest blessing."
My family is the one who brings out my best in every situation in life.They are
the ones who trust me, support me, and guide me no matter what to be able
to experience and build a life without worry. They are important to me as they
are the ones who nurtured my growth throughout my life.

Friends

My friends are the ones who bring out the fun in me. They let me explore the
wonders of the world with happiness and good intentions. They are the ones who
contributed to building my self-esteem and confidence, and they always got my back whenever I was carrying the weight of the world.

Love

The one I'm on right now is currently giving me serotonin and dopamine, which
helps me remember every day of my worth as a person. Being able to experience
such things lets me view perspective in a bigger picture in relationships.

Me

I consider myself one of the important people in my life because without me,
this life is completely nothing–just a blast of darkness. I have been loving myself
ever since and I’m continually on a venture to know myself more and explore my
strengths and weaknesses that can help me accept myself in the coming years.
